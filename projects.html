Currently Building

bubbl! is the active product build.

bubbl! is an accessibility-focused mobile communication app designed for deaf and hard-of-hearing users. It uses live speech streaming, face tracking, and anchored caption overlays to keep the transcript visually connected to the person speaking instead of forcing attention down to a detached subtitle region.

bubbl!

A real-time communication interface built around the speaker.

The app combines Kotlin, Jetpack Compose, CameraX, ML Kit face tracking, speech streaming, dynamic caption motion, contextual cues, and accessibility settings into a calm conversation-first interface.
